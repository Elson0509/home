Hobby

In addition to programming, when I have free time I dedicate my efforts in the woodworking to create furniture for myself and friends. You can see below some of my work.

After a couple years my son was born, I created this bed for him inspired by the character Batman. I added some lights to create an effect. A video of this bed can be seen on youtube.

After my son's toys were scattered around the house, I decided to make this big chest to organize them.

A friend's computer desk was getting old, so he asked me to make a brand new one. The work was great.

A friend had a dresser to be restored. After a long and delicate work, the result was perfect. Detail: the dresser was very worn because it was 70 years old! You can see it by clicking here.
